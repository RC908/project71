Campus Life

school church

was established in 1976

The church is a staple of our school. It was the original building where we built this school. The church is older than our school and a proud part of our history.



school library

was built in 1980

The library was built right after the school finished construction, and it has hundreds of books for all ages. The Library has many systems to ensure all boks are brought back in good condition.



school auditorium

was built in 1982

The auditorium was made to hold big announcements and honor those who are represnting the school in different tournaments. It can hold all 578 students and faculty members inside.

What's New

  • New School Canteen

    Our new canteen is much more organised than it used to be. We now used an online menu to let students choose what they want for lunch and where they are sitting, and then that data gets sent to the kitchen, where they hand out the food where the order was made. At the end of the day, we use all of this information to prepare a certain amount of food and not waste as much.
